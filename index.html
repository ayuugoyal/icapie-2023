About the Conference

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University East Delhi Campus

"In the year 1998, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) emerged as a pioneering institution, borne from the visionary aspirations of the Government of NCT of Delhi and crafted within the legislative framework of the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University Act, 1998. This legislative foundation, further fortified by amendments in 1999, set the stage for a remarkable journey. Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 12B of the UGC Act, GGSIPU embarked on its mission. GGSIPU isn't just an educational institution; it's a testament to excellence. Accredited with a CGPA of 3.56 on a seven-point scale and holding an A++ Grade, its distinction is valid for an impressive 7-year span, commencing on the 14th of February, 2023. GGSIPU's purpose is to nurture and champion studies, research, and extension work in emerging areas of higher education. The focus remains squarely on professional education, spanning fields such as engineering, technology, management studies, medicine, pharmacy, nursing, education, law, and more. The university's pursuit extends to achieving excellence across these domains and related matters. GGSIPU doesn't just educate; it inspires. It fuels the hearts and minds of scholars, empowering them to contribute to the greater good of society. It equips them to adapt to the evolving needs of the economy, fosters cultural leadership, and strives for peace, harmony, and prosperity for all. Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University aspires to deliver market-oriented professional education to the students of India, with a special emphasis on Delhi. It aims to serve the cause of higher education and cater to the demands of Indian industries by establishing colleges and Schools of Studies as Center of Excellence in emerging fields. These fields prominently include engineering, technology, medicine, education, pharmacy, nursing, law, and more.

GGS Indraprastha University's East Campus is a shining example of the university's dedication to providing a world-class learning experience. This campus seamlessly blends aesthetics with cutting-edge technology, creating an environment conducive to holistic development. As India strides into the fourth Industrial Revolution, GGSIPU stands ready to meet the challenge. The University has recently inaugurated two new Schools of Studies at its East Campus, namely, the University School of Automation & Robotics (USAR) and the University School of Design & Innovation (USDI) in Surajmal Vihar, New Delhi. These state-of-the-art facilities, equipped with labs and studios, offer programs in emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Industrial Internet of Things, Automation, and Robotics in USAR, and a Bachelor of Design in USDI. GGSIPU's focus extends beyond education; it's about empowering students with entrepreneurial and employability skills, ensuring they are well-prepared for the demands of the modern world."

8th International Conference, ICAPIE-2023

8th International Conference on Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (ICAPIE - 2023) is being organized by Centre for Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (CAPIER), Delhi Technological University (DTU), Delhi, India in collaboration with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (East Delhi Campus), India during December 11-12, 2023 (https://icapie2023.netlify.app/). CAPIE DTU has made exponential growth since its inception, providing a forum to the academia & research institutions, industry experts designers management professionals, engineers and executives to collaborate and explore new windows of discovery, innovation and advancements for a sustainable future. The conference is also aimed to promote synergetic partnership between academia and industry and also to showcase the growth of cutting edge technology developed in the field of Production and Industrial Engineering.¬† The 8th International Conference, ICAPIE-2022, provides a forum to the best talents from the academia & research institutions, industry experts, designers, management professionals, engineers and executives to collaborate and address the current challenges, to explore new windows for discovery, innovation and advancements, promoting the contribution of Production and Industrial Engineering & Technology for a sustainable future.

The ICAPIE-2019, ICAPIE-2021 and ICAPIE-2022 proved grant success and the most of the papers have been published by Lecture Notes in Mechanical Engineering (LNME), a Scopus indexed series by Springer. In addition, the special issues are published in the following SCIE indexed journals, Indian Journal of Engineering and Materials Sciences, 2020, Journal of Engineering Research, 2022, Indian Journal of Engineering and Materials Sciences, 2022.

ICAPIE-2023 invites participation of Academicians, Researchers, Engineers, stakeholders, and Industrialists form the areas (but not limited to) of Mechanical Engineering, Materials Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, Instrumentation, and other related areas (https://icapie2023.netlify.app/). Conference Theme

Join us at the GGS Indrapratha University- East Campus for the International Conference on Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (ICAPIE-2023). Our conference brings together experts, researchers, and industry leaders to explore emerging areas such as production, industrial engineering, industry 4.O, machine learning, data science, artificial intelligence, Big data, Optimization, E-Manufacturing and many more allied areas shaping the future of production and industrial engineering. With a focus on cutting-edge research, transformative applications, and collaborative discussions, this event is a must-attend for anyone passionate about the evolution of modern manufacturing and its impact on industry.

Objectives:

  • Keynote talks by industry experts.
  • Industry and research focused themes with high stake panel sessions and presentations.
  • The Convergence of AI, IoT, and Robotics in Smart Manufacturing.
  • To foster a platform for the exchange of knowledge, ideas, and experiences among researchers, academics, and industry professionals in the field of advanced production and industrial engineering.
  • To showcase the latest technological advancements and research findings in the domain of manufacturing, with a focus on their practical applications and implications for global industries.
  • To facilitate collaboration and networking among participants to encourage interdisciplinary research and innovative solutions to contemporary manufacturing challenges.
  • To enhance the understanding of Industry 4.0 technologies and their potential to transform the manufacturing landscape, and to identify strategies for their successful implementation.
  • To provide a platform for the exchange of ideas and experiences among researchers.

Call for papers:

This conference serves as a dynamic platform for the exchange of cutting-edge research, insights, and innovative ideas in the realm of manufacturing and industrial engineering. We welcome high-quality papers spanning a wide range of topics, from Industry 4.0 and sustainable manufacturing to quality control, digital transformation, and workforce development. The two day conference brings together master class experts, keynote speakers, country chair, delegates from academia and industry, panel members, doctoral students, and organising faculty members to shape the conversation on technology, sustainability, industry, and analytics.
