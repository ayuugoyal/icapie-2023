About the Conference

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University

Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) is the first University established in 1998 by Govt. of NCT of Delhi under the provisions of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University Act, 1998 read with its Amendment in 1999 The University is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC), India under section 12B of UGC Act.

The Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University has been Accredited with a CGPA of 3.56 on a seven point scale at A++ Grade valid for a period of 7 years from 14-02-2023.

It is a teaching and affiliating University with the explicit objective of facilitating and promoting “studies, research and extension work in emerging areas of higher education with focus on professional education, for example engineering, technology, management studies, medicine, pharmacy, nursing, education, law, etc. and also to achieve excellence in these and connected fields and other matters connected therewith or incidental thereto.”

“The University will stimulate both the hearts and minds of scholars, empower them to contribute to the welfare of society at large; train them to adopt themselves to the changing needs of the economy; advocate them for cultural leadership to ensure peace, harmony and prosperity for all.”

“Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University shall strive hard to provide a market-oriented professional education to the student community of India in general and of Delhi in particular, to serve the cause of higher education as well as to meet the needs of the Indian industries by promoting establishment of colleges and Schools of Studies as Centres of Excellence in emerging areas of education with focus on professional education in disciplines of engineering, technology, medicine, education, pharmacy, nursing, law, etc.”

8th International Conference, ICAPIE-2023

8th International Conference on Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (ICAPIE - 2023) is being organized by Centre for Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering (CAPIER), Delhi Technological University (DTU), Delhi, India in collaboration with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (East Delhi Campus), India during December 11-12, 2023 (https://icapie2023.netlify.app/). The 8th International Conference, ICAPIE-2022, provides a forum to the best talents from the academia & research institutions, industry experts, designers, management professionals, engineers and executives to collaborate and address the current challenges, to explore new windows for discovery, innovation and advancements, promoting the contribution of Production and Industrial Engineering & Technology for a sustainable future. The conference is also aimed to promote synergetic - vibrant partnership between academia and industry and also to showcase the growth of cutting edge technology developed in the field of Production and Industrial Engineering in the recent past.

The ICAPIE-2019, ICAPIE-2021 and ICAPIE-2022 have been published by Lecture Notes in Mechanical Engineering (LNME), a Scopus indexed series by Springer. In addition, the special issues are published in the past in the following SCIE indexed journals, Indian Journal of Engineering and Materials Sciences, 2020, Journal of Engineering Research, 2022, Indian Journal of Engineering and Materials Sciences, 2022.

ICAPIE-2023 invites contributions from the stakeholders from the areas (but not limited to) of Mechanical Engineering, Materials Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, Instrumentation and other related areas (https://icapie2023.netlify.app/). The peer-reviewed accepted, registered and presented papers will be published in the Scopus Indexed Series/ Journals. Papers can be submitted to the conference through:

Conference Theme

Join us at the University School of Automation & Robotics for the International Conference on Advanced Production and Industrial Engineering, where we delve into the forefront of technological innovation. Our conference brings together experts, researchers, and industry leaders to explore emerging areas such as production, industrial engineering, industry 4.O, machine learning, data science, artificial intelligence, Big data, Optimization, E-Manufacturing and many more allied areas shaping the future of production and industrial engineering. With a focus on cutting-edge research, transformative applications, and collaborative discussions, this event is a must-attend for anyone passionate about the evolution of modern manufacturing and its impact on industry.

Objectives:

  • Keynote talks by industry experts.
  • Industry and research focused themes with high stake panel sessions and presentations.
  • The Convergence of AI, IoT, and Robotics in Smart Manufacturing.
  • To foster a platform for the exchange of knowledge, ideas, and experiences among researchers, academics, and industry professionals in the field of advanced production and industrial engineering.
  • To showcase the latest technological advancements and research findings in the domain of manufacturing, with a focus on their practical applications and implications for global industries.
  • To facilitate collaboration and networking among participants to encourage interdisciplinary research and innovative solutions to contemporary manufacturing challenges.
  • To enhance the understanding of Industry 4.0 technologies and their potential to transform the manufacturing landscape, and to identify strategies for their successful implementation.
  • To provide a platform for the exchange of ideas and experiences among researchers.

Call for papers:

This conference serves as a dynamic platform for the exchange of cutting-edge research, insights, and innovative ideas in the realm of manufacturing and industrial engineering. We welcome high-quality papers spanning a wide range of topics, from Industry 4.0 and sustainable manufacturing to quality control, digital transformation, and workforce development. The two day conference brings together master class experts, keynote speakers, country chair, delegates from academia and industry, panel members, doctoral students, and organising faculty members to shape the conversation on technology, sustainability, industry, and analytics.
