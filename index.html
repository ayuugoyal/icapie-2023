About the Conference

8th International Conference, ICAPIE-2023

The 8th International Conference, ICAPIE-2023, provides a forum to the best talents from the academia & research institutions, industry experts, designers, management professionals, engineers and executives to collaborate and address the current challenges, in order to explore new windows for discovery, innovation and advancements, promoting the contribution of Production and Industrial Engineering & Technology for a sustainable future.

The conference is also aimed to promote synergetic - vibrant partnership between academia and industry and also to showcase the growth of cutting edge technology developed in the field of Automation, Robotics, Design & Innovation, Production and Industrial Engineering in the recent past.

ICAPIE-2023 invites contributions from the stakeholders from the areas (but not limited to) of Production Engineering, Materials Engineering, Manufacturing Engineering, Design, Management, Instrumentation, Computers, Electronics etc...

Call for Papers

Publishers:

Selected full papers can be submitted to the following journals. The paper will go through the normal review process of the journal but they will be acknowledged as papers presented at ICAM-2024. The journals are:

  • High-quality papers can be submitted for peer review and possible publication in Science and Technology of Welding and Joining (Taylor and Francis)
  • The top standard selected paper will be published in the Journal of Micromanufacturing as Special Issue " Additive Micromanufacturing"
  • The top standard Selected paper likely to be considered for the publication in International Journal on Interactive Design and Manufacturing (IJIDeM)
  • Paper in a book: All the selected full papers (after review) (about 8-15 pages) can be submitted to be published in Lecture Notes in Mechanical Engineering (Scopus Indexed) Springer proceedings book volume with ISBN number. Springer will conduct quality checks on the accepted papers and only papers that pass these checks will be published.

Conference Scope:

The scope of the conference includes, but is not limited to:

  • Kinematics and Dynamics of machines, Compliant Mechanisms
  • Gear, Cams and power transmission systems
  • Mechanisms and Machines for Rural, Agricultural and Industrial Applications
  • Mechanisms for Space Applications
  • Mechanisms for Energy Harvesting
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Human Centric Robotics
  • Man–Machine System, Mechatronics and Micro–Mechanisms
  • CAD, CAGD
